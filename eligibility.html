BLOOD DONATION ELIGIBILITY

Below is a list of basic requirements and guidelines for donating blood. If you are not sure of your eligibility to donate blood, contact Donor Support at 800-747-5401 Ã—1281.

Eligibility Guidelines

Basic Requirements

  • Age: 18 to 60 years
  • Weight: Minimum 50 kg
  • Hemoglobin: At least 12.5 g/dl

Health Conditions

  • Must be in good health on the day of donation.
  • Should be free from infections (HIV, Hepatitis, etc.).
  • No recent history of fever, cold, or flu.

Time Intervals

  • Minimum gap between whole blood donations:
    • Male donors: Every 3 months
    • Female donors: Every 4 months

Restrictions (Cannot Donate If...)

  • Had major surgery in the last 6 months.
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding.
  • Recently got a tattoo or piercing (within 6 months).
  • Taking certain medications (e.g., antibiotics, blood thinners).
  • Consumed alcohol within the last 24 hours.
