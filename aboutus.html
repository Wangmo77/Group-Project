About B-Link Samtse

Connecting blood donors with those in need throughout the Samtse community. Our mission is to ensure no one suffers due to lack of blood availability when they need it most.

Our Mission

B-Link Samtse was established to address the critical need for a reliable blood donation system in the Samtse district. We recognized that many patients and their families faced challenges in finding compatible blood donors during emergencies.

Our platform bridges the gap between voluntary blood donors and recipients, creating a community of lifesavers. We believe that everyone in Samtse should have access to safe blood when they need it, regardless of their location or circumstances.

Through our easy-to-use registration system, donors can sign up and be notified when their blood type is needed. Those requiring blood can submit requests that are immediately circulated to compatible donors in our network.

We work closely with local hospitals and health centers to ensure that the donation process is safe, efficient, and meets all medical standards.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every donor and recipient with empathy, understanding the urgency and emotional weight of blood needs.

Community

We believe in the power of community coming together to support one another in times of need.

Safety

We prioritize the health and safety of both donors and recipients through rigorous screening and protocols.

Efficiency

We streamline the process of connecting donors with those in need, reducing response times during emergencies.

How It Works

1

Register

Sign up as a blood donor by providing your basic information and blood type. The process is simple and takes just a few minutes.

2

Get Notified

When someone in Samtse needs your blood type, you'll receive a notification with details about the request.

3

Donate

Visit the designated health center at your convenience to donate blood and help save a life in your community.

4

Request Blood

If you or someone you know needs blood, fill out our request form and we'll connect you with compatible donors.
