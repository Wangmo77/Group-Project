Success Stories

Real stories of hope, resilience, and lives saved through the generosity of blood donors in Samtse

Featured Stories

A Life Saved During Emergency Surgery

At 78, I thought my time was over. Strangers gave me their blood, and now I have more mornings to watch the sun rise over the mountains. They gave an old man the most precious gift: more time.

A Mother's Second Chance

After the complicated birth of my son, I was losing hope as I lost blood. The doctors worked tirelessly, but it was the gift from four strangers that truly saved me. I am here today to watch my child grow because of them. To the silent heroes who shared their life with me, my family is forever in your debt.

The Grateful Donor-Turned-Recipient

As a lifelong donor, I never imagined needing blood myself. After receiving 4 units, I'm alive to raise my children. Thank you for this incredible gift of life. I will forever champion the cause I once only supported.

A Community's Lifeline

When our grandfather needed rare blood for surgery, our community and strangers answered the call. Their donations gave us more precious years with him. We will never forget their kindness.

A Survivor's Pledge

As a thalassemia patient, I've relied on blood donors my entire life. Their silent gifts allowed me to have a future. I now advocate for donation to pay their kindness forward.

A Father's Gratitude

When our son needed blood during his treatment, strangers answered the call. He is now back at school, laughing and learning, because of you. From our family to yours, thank you for saving our child's future.

A Parent's Thanks

When my son Tashi was diagnosed with leukemia, his tiny body couldn't fight alone. He needed blood transfusions regularly to survive the treatment. We watched the donated blood flow into his veins, and with it, color returned to his cheeks and strength to his body. The anonymous donors didn't just give blood; they gave us back our smiling, energetic boy. They are our family's angels.

Our Privacy Commitment

At B-Link Samtse, we respect the privacy and confidentiality of both blood donors and recipients. Our processes ensure that personal information is protected while still allowing us to share inspiring stories of hope and recovery.

Confidentiality Guaranteed

Recipients never know the identity of their blood donors, and donors never know who received their blood. This ensures complete privacy for all parties involved.

How We Collect Stories

We receive messages of gratitude from recipients through hospital staff who act as intermediaries. All identifying information is removed before stories are shared.

Voluntary Participation

Story sharing is completely voluntary. Recipients who wish to express gratitude can do so through anonymous messages that we share with donor encouragement.

Data Protection

All personal information is stored securely and separately from donation records. We follow strict data protection protocols to ensure privacy.
