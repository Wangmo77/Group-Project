Why Donate Blood?

Blood is needed every two seconds. About 1 in 7 people entering a hospital need blood. Your donation can save up to three lives.

Eligibility Criteria

Generally, donors must be in good health, at least 17 years old, and weigh at least 110 pounds. Learn more about eligibility requirements.
